    * Penguin: "Where will we get penguins? Seems expensive."
    * Polar Bear: "Large and potentially dangerous; logistical nightmare."
    * Ice Fairy: "Pretty, but fragile and easily broken; manufacturing costs are high."
    * Yeti: "Hard to find; unmanageable; also potentially dangerous."
* **Persona 2 (The Visionary):**
    * Snowman: "Whimsical and nostalgic."
    * Penguin: "Charming and represents Antarctic wonder."
    * Polar Bear: "Majestic embodies the Arctic spirit."
    * Ice Fairy: "Ethereal, captivating, and embodies winter magic."
    * Yeti: "Mysterious and encourages a sense of adventure."
* **Persona 3 (The Systems Thinker):**
    * Snowman: "Dependent on temperature; unsustainable."
    * Penguin: "Represents climate change awareness."
    * Polar Bear: "Iconic symbol of Arctic conservation."
    * Ice Fairy: "Promotes environmental responsibility, as they are tied to the health of the environment."
    * Yeti: "A reminder of the unknown and the importance of respecting wildlife."
* **Persona 4 (The Optimist):**
    * Snowman: "A jolly symbol of winter fun."
    * Penguin: "Playful and endearing!"
    * Polar Bear: "Strong, cuddly, and lovable!"
    * Ice Fairy: "Magical and enchanting!"
    * Yeti: "Unique and exciting!"
* **Persona 5 (The Devil's Advocate):**
    * Snowman: "Snowmen are clich√©, and melt."

    * Polar Bear: "Polar Bears are apex predators; scary."

    * Yeti: "Yeti are terrifying monsters from nightmares"
* **Persona 6 (The Mediator):**
    * Snowman: "A classic choice, but easily improved upon."
    * Penguin: "A solid middle-ground option."
    * Polar Bear: "A strong contender with broad appeal."
    * Ice Fairy: "Brings elegance and wonder but may not resonate with everyone."
    * Yeti: "A wildcard choice that could be very successful or a complete disaster."
* **Persona 7 (The User Advocate):**
    * Snowman: "Timeless appeal to all ages; kids love snowmen."
    * Penguin: "Cute, cuddly, and educational."
    * Polar Bear: "Powerful and impressive!"
    * Ice Fairy: "Girls adore fairies!"
    * Yeti: "Mysterious, cool, but maybe too scary?"
* **Persona 8 (The Traditionalist):**
    * Snowman: "A long-standing winter tradition."

    * Ice Fairy: "Mythical creatures from winter folklore"
    * Yeti: "Stories for daring children"

    * Polar Bear: "High recognition and positive sentiment."
    * Ice Fairy: "Rising popularity due to fantasy trends"
    * Yeti: "Niche interest; polarizing."

## PHASE 2 (Rebuttal):

* **Devil's Advocate:**
    * Polar Bear: "Polar bears are terrifying creatures! We're going to tell kids this giant predator is their 'friend'?"
    * Ice Fairy: "Ice Fairies are unrealistic and perpetuate harmful, impossible beauty standards."
* **The Pragmatist:**
    * Polar Bear: "A very large costume and the care and feeding of live bears is expensive. Can we clean it when it gets dirty?"
    * Ice Fairy: "How fragile can the costume be? Will we need an army of seamstresses on hand to fix it?"

## PHASE 3 (Vote):

* **Persona 1 (The Pragmatist):** Penguin "It's more cost-effective."
* **Persona 2 (The Visionary):** Ice Fairy "Inspires wonder and magic."
* **Persona 3 (The Systems Thinker):** Polar Bear "Represents environmental issues."
* **Persona 4 (The Optimist):** Ice Fairy "Creates joy!"
* **Persona 5 (The Devil's Advocate):** Yeti "At least it's unique!"
* **Persona 6 (The Mediator):** Polar Bear "Balances tradition with modern concerns."
* **Persona 7 (The User Advocate):** Ice Fairy "Kids love them!"
* **Persona 8 (The Traditionalist):** Snowman "A timeless classic!"
* **Persona 9 (The Data Analyst):** Polar Bear "Data trends show positive sentiment."

## PHASE 4 (Synthesis):

Tallying the votes:

* Polar Bear: 3
* Ice Fairy: 3
* Penguin: 1
* Yeti: 1
* Snowman: 1

We have a tie between Polar Bear and Ice Fairy.

Since there is a tie, we will have the Mediator choose between the remaining top choices.
The Mediator (Persona 6) chooses the Polar Bear. The justification is "The Polar Bear has both tradition and ecological concern. It would be my deciding vote."

**The Winner is Polar Bear.**
